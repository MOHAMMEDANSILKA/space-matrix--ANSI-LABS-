## 🔩 Screwing Mechanisms & Internal Mounting
When you buy an off-the-shelf project box, the outer lid screws are usually included. However, for mounting your internal components or ensuring reusable threads, consider these mechanisms:

### 1. Self-Tapping Screws
*   **Best For:** Simple, permanent assembly in plastic cases.
*   **How it works:** The internal plastic casing features hollow pillars. A small self-tapping screw cuts its own thread directly into the plastic as you drive it down.

### 2. Brass Threaded Inserts
*   **Best For:** Heavy-use cases that need to be opened and closed frequently (e.g., battery replacement).
*   **How it works:** Plastic threads strip easily over time. You press a small **M2 or M3 brass threaded insert** into the plastic hole using a hot soldering iron tip. The plastic melts slightly around it to lock it in place, giving you a permanent, reusable metal screw track.

### 3. Hardware Kits
*   **Sourcing:** You can buy single assortment kits containing hundreds of M2 and M3 micro-screws, nuts, and brass inserts on Amazon India, Robu, or AliExpress to easily match whatever enclosure depth you choose.
